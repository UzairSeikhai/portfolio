Tunescribers

  • Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, PHP, Laravel, PayPal
  • Project Link: Tunescribers

Tunescriber is a web-based application where we sell tune scripts created by tune providers to tune customers. Developed using the Laravel PHP framework, the platform features three distinct dashboards for customers, providers, and admins, enhancing user experience and management capabilities. Customers can make payments through PayPal and various credit or debit card options at different locations. The application includes an "Add to Cart" feature, allowing tune providers to sell their scripts to both guest and registered customers. Additionally, a discount coupon option is available for customers to avail discounts on their purchases.
