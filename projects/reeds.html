REEDS

  • Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PHP, Laravel, WOPI, Multitenancy
  • Project Link: REEDS

We have successfully developed a cloud application for Reeds, enhancing the law firm’s efficiency and productivity through key modules. The Practice Management Module centralizes operations such as case management, document tracking, and scheduling, resulting in improved organization and collaboration. A significant challenge in this project was the WOPI Integration, which facilitates the use of Microsoft Office applications (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint) within a web-based environment. This integration required finding a solution that allows seamless interoperability between our PHP-based application and Microsoft Office, primarily developed in ASP.NET.

The Finance Management Module provides comprehensive financial tracking, invoicing, and reporting, enabling effective financial management and accurate billing. Integration with Microsoft Services enhances productivity by facilitating document sharing and communication within the Microsoft ecosystem.

Robust workflow structures, integrations, and permissions ensure smooth information flow, streamlined processes, and controlled access to confidential data, promoting better organization, productivity, and data security. Another significant feature of this application is Multitenancy, which allows Reeds to provide tailored solutions to various companies by creating their own subdomains. To manage these companies and their hosting details, we developed a separate application named Configuration Manager.

Key Points

  • Contributed to the development of a multi-tenant platform tailored for UK law firms, collaborating with a team of 15–18 engineers to deliver scalable and robust solutions.
  • Designed and implemented a WOPI (Web Application Open Platform Interface) package in Laravel, enabling seamless document integration and editing within the platform, improving efficiency for legal professionals.
  • Focused on building API-driven solutions, ensuring compatibility and scalability across multiple tenants while maintaining data security and isolation.
  • Delivered features aligned with client requirements, enhancing operational workflows and modernizing legal document management systems.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams to ensure the platform met compliance and performance standards critical for the legal industry.
